About Otis AI

We're on a mission to democratize presentation creation by making it accessible, intelligent, and effortless for everyone. Otis AI is a presentation builder that uses AI to create stunning presentations in seconds. Join thousands of professionals who trust Otis for their presentations.

Our Story

Born from a Simple Problem

Otis was founded in 2024 when our team realized that creating professional presentations was unnecessarily complex and time-consuming. We saw brilliant ideas getting lost in poor design and hours of formatting work.

We believed that everyone should be able to create stunning presentations that match their ideas, regardless of their design skills or available time.

Today, Otis has helped thousands of professionals, entrepreneurs, and teams transform their ideas into compelling presentations in seconds, not hours.
