Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create my first presentation? +

Creating your first presentation is easy! Simply sign up for an account, choose a template or start from scratch, enter your content, and let our AI generate a professional presentation for you. The entire process takes just a few minutes.

What file formats can I export my presentations to? +

You can export your presentations to PowerPoint (.pptx), PDF, and Google Slides formats. We're constantly working on adding more export options based on user feedback.

Is my data secure with Otis AI? +

Absolutely! We take data security seriously. All your presentations and data are encrypted and stored securely. We never share your content with third parties without your explicit consent.

Can I collaborate with team members on presentations? +

Yes! Our team collaboration features allow you to invite team members, share presentations, and work together in real-time. You can set different permission levels for each team member.

What's included in the free plan? +

Our free plan includes 3 presentations per month, access to basic templates, and standard export options. It's perfect for trying out Otis AI and creating simple presentations.

How do I cancel my subscription? +

You can cancel your subscription at any time from your account settings. Your account will remain active until the end of your current billing period, and you'll retain access to all your presentations.

Still need help?

Get in touch with our support team and we'll get back to you within 24 hours.
